About

ReddID is a powerful social tool to encourage the rewarding of positive social well-being.

Bridging multiple social networks, ReddID allows seamless one-button tipping between users on different networks, while removing the security and theft risks of centralized tipbots, questionable websites and anonymous administrators. With all data stored on the blockchain and secured by thousands of staking users, Reddcoin's PoSV algorithm ensures proof against hacking and theft. Visit www.reddcoin.com for details
